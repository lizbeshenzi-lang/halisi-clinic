Who We Are

Medicine the Way It Should Be: Personal

Halisi — meaning "genuine" in Swahili — was founded in 2017 on the belief that quality healthcare shouldn't require travelling to Westlands or paying international fees. We brought specialist-level care to Kilimani and made it accessible.

Every patient at Halisi is seen by name, not by number. Our doctors take time. Our nurses follow up. And when you need a specialist, we have the network to get you there — fast.
